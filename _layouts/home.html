Hi, I'm David Offereins Over the last 15 years I have designed brands, campaigns, digital products and more. I am a Jack of all trades & a master of some.

I create brands and campaigns that not only look good, but effectively communicate. Design can make a brand feel premium, or upbeat and happy, or empowering. Together with the client, I define the key message that the brand should deliver. I then use colors, typography, shapes and images to effectively communicate that message.

I enjoy working with oldschool print media, but today I work mostly with code. I enjoy building thoughtful, intuitive interfaces, often based on complex datasets. Click here for some examples of my work.

I studied Geography and after working some years in that field, I went to the Royal Academy of Art in The Hague to study grapic design. At first I worked mostly on urban development projects, but later shifted towards tech-companies. I then joined cybersecurity start-up Cybersprint as Creative Director, where I was responsible for everything design related, including branding and product design. In 2022 Cybersprint was acquired by Darktrace, not long thereafter I went back to freelancing. Check my full resume here.

I am currently working for another startup, designing the brand and the product interface. Besides that, I am available for interesting projects. So feel free to get in touch!
